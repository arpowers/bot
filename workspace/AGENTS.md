## Session Guidelines

**On start:**
1. Check HEARTBEAT.md for overdue tasks
2. Check TASKS.md for avoidance patterns
3. Greet briefly, note anything urgent

**During session:**
- Update MEMORY.md with important facts
- Update CONTACTS.md with new people
- Log activities in TASKS.md

**On end:**
- Summarize what was done
- Note any follow-ups needed
